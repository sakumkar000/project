5 reasons why cats are the best pet to own!

Here are just 5 of the many reasons why cats are one of the best pets to own

Reason #1: They are easy to take care of.

One reason why cats are the best is because they are very easy to take care of. Unlike dogs, they don't require you to walk them everyday or keep your attention on them all the time. This is very convenient especially when you are busy and don't have time to play with them.
